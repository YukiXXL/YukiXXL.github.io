INFORMATION

The tips and tricks here apply for both Gamecube and PS2 versions.

Tips and Tricks

Tips

- No need to kill all the enemies if wanting to get the door open. This is EXTREMELY IMPORTANT FOR SPEEDRUNNERS!

- Anything else that applies for classics applies for this one aswell.

- Setting Friendly Fire option in COOP to OFF or REVERSE nerfs any enemy fire attack that deals massive damage to the player.

- If a player respawns on a level with a vehicle (jeep), the vehicle also respawns with full health and can be found in it's original position.


Tricks

Switching weapons trick

Switching the weapon while it's reloading skips the reload animation. When switched back, it comes back to the idle stance.

This is extremly useful for Sniper Rifle which is one of the most powerful weapons.

How to do it?

Simply while the weapon is going to reload, switch to another weapon and then switch back.

This also works for Double-Barrel Shotgun and Serious Bomb but the timing has to be right.

For other, switch as fast as you can. It works on: Grenade Launcher, Sniper Rifle and Cannon.


Checkpoint trick

Some checkpoints get triggered before the fight is over. This makes it useful for speedrunning.

How to do it?

For example, on Via Aurelia, there is a position where early checkpoint can be triggered. It's in front of the barn with Juvenile Werebulls, in the middle where usually 3 items spawn ( 2 HP and 1 ARMOR ).

After triggering the checkpoint and respawning to it will skip all the fights before like they were finished.
